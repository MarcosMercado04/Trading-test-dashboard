Why the survivor works

When NQ opens weak and keeps falling hard through the first hour, the move continues into the close. Downside is self-reinforcing — forced selling (margin calls, risk-parity de-grossing, stop cascades) and dealer-gamma hedging amplify a weak open. There's no symmetric forced-buying engine, so the edge is short-only and strongest on the highest-beta, most options-driven index: NQ (it does not transfer to ES, Dow, or gold).
